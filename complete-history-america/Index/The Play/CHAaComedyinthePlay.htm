Comedy in the Play

 

 

As with all of their productions, the playwrights have drawn on numerous comedy styles in creating The Complete History of America (abridged).  The time during which they grew up (see Context of Boomer Humor) proved to be a fertile one for aspiring comics, presenting numerous sources and styles. 

 

Yet focusing on the “Boomer” origins without noting the specific, historically based comedy styles underplays the creativity of the playwrights.  The links below will take you to various discussions on comedy styles employed in the play.
