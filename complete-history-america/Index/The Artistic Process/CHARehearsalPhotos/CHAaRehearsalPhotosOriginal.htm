Dress Parade

 

Dress Parade is the time in the rehearsal process when the director and actors see for the first time all of the costumes.  Discussion sometimes leads to requests from the actors (e.g., for more room in a particular garment) and the director (please try another color or style).  Many of the costumes in Complete History are not full costumes, but rather only wigs or hats.

Tech

 

During the final week before preview performances, the crew goes through “tech,” the time when the technical aspects of the show—lighting, sound, costumes, props—are brought together on stage with the actors.  This production has many, many props and costume changes, which places a premium on the backstage runners and dressers being organized and in control of the actors as they arrive from one scene to get ready for the next.  There are 17 scenes (see Synopsis).
